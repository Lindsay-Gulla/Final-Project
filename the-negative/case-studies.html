Case Studies

 

Being a huge fan a Family Guy, I often do not take offense to the jokes being made. However, there are a few jokes in particular that stick out in my mind because Family Guy took it too far. Brian is talking about his recent accomplishment and Chris says: “yeah that’s a great achievement, just like the first chemotherapy patient to figure out the handkerchief look.” The screen then cuts away to a sick man putting on a bandana and he yells to his wife: “Honey get in here.” She sees him and says “oh my god.” And at the same time they both say: “it’s like you don’t even have cancer.” Then the sick man says: “no but I still have pretty aggressive cancer” (Macfarlane, 2008, “Play it Again, Brian”).
