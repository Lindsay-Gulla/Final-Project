The Negative Aspects within Humor

Ethics play a huge part in humor; some comedians believe it is their ethical duty to make jokes especially when it comes to politicians or current events. Humor is a way for comedians to express their opinion on certain issues and present it in a lighter fashion. Humor is objective, what we find funny is based on that specific joke not our own opinion.

Being a huge fan a Family Guy, I often do not take offense to the jokes being made. However, there are a few jokes in particular that stick out in my mind because Family Guy took it too far.

These are examples of humor’s attempt to cope with tragedy failing. These types of jokes are usually not found funny, but most people sit dumbfounded thinking how could this air on TV. When the joke is too close to the event for actual comfort, it becomes offensive.
