Humor: History, The Positive, and The Negative

 

As instinct would allow us, humans have learned to deal or cope with certain issues through the use of humor. This is especially true for a group of people, a family or a nation, in that humor can be a force of unity. For example, people who are overweight will often make fat jokes, or someone who is of a certain stereotyped race, will make jokes about their skin color or racial origins.

This website represents my research on humor, how it has evolved from historic roots, where humor was banned in many places, and why it is more useful in society now. After tragedy strikes, there is a process of first grieving and then unification, humor comes in second, but is extremely effective as coping mechanism.
